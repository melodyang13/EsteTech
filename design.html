2023's Hottest Interior Design Trends in the Philippines

Discover the newest developments in the realm of interior design as we delve into the foremost trends that are molding Filipino households this year.

01 Personalization

Individuals are progressively searching for methods to ensure their residences mirror their own distinct fashion and character. This implies integrating personal details, such as inherited possessions, art, and pictures, into the arrangement.

02 Living in color

After a few years of understated earth tones, hue is making a comeback in interior design. Striking and lively colors are being utilized to craft fashionable and attention-grabbing spaces.

03 More with wallpaper

Wallpaper is no more viewed as an outdated trend. In reality, it is progressively gaining popularity as a means to incorporate individuality and fascination into walls. Striking designs and textures are in vogue, but even uncomplicated, plain shades can create a significant effect.

04 Closed floor plans

Open floor schemes were all the fury a few years ago, but they are starting to yield more enclosed floor schemes. This is partly due to the reality that individuals seek more seclusion and division in their residences.

05 Neutral Colors

Neutral tones are still trendy, but they are adopting a gentler and more organic appearance in 2023. Tints of ivory, tan, and chestnut are being employed to fashion soothing and serene environments.

06 Medium to dark wood

Wood is another trendy substance in interior design, and medium-to-dark shades are in vogue in 2023. These shades generate a feeling of coziness and elegance in the residence.

07 Heritage & Vintage

Retro and old furniture is becoming progressively popular as individuals search for means to include personality and past in their residences. These items can be discovered at second-hand markets, old-fashioned shops, and on the internet.

08 Bold and Marble

Marble, an everlasting substance that never goes out of fashion, holds an enduring appeal. This entails employing marble in grandiose and captivating manners, exemplified by its use in expansive counter tops, magnificent flooring, and striking fireplace surrounds.
